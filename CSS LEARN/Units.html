Bokaro Steel City (About this soundpronunciation (help·info)), or simply Bokaro, is one of the planned cities of India and also an urban agglomeration, a major industrial centre and the fourth-largest city in the Indian state of Jharkhand. It is the administrative headquarters of Bokaro district.
